Empowering Health and Safety in the Workplace

Join us for a transformative 3-day course designed for UFCW 401 members passionate about workplace health and safety. In partnership with the Alberta Workers’ Health Centre, this course deepens your understanding of your responsibilities as a member and your critical role within the Joint Health and Safety Committee (JHSC).

Course Highlights

  • Explore Alberta’s essential legislation governing Occupational Health and Safety (OHS)
  • Discover resources to support your advocacy for safer work environments
  • Build confidence to contribute effectively within the JHSC

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for any UFCW 401 member eager to champion health and safety initiatives—whether you are new to the topic or looking to enhance your knowledge.
