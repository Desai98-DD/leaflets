First-aid

First-aid training is crucial for union members as it enables them to respond effectively to workplace injuries and medical emergencies before professional help arrives. It supports workplace safety and helps reduce the severity of incidents.

Our First-Aid training covers all Red Cross criteria for first aid, including:

  • CPR
  • AED training
  • Choking
  • Poisons
  • Wound care
  • Environmental emergencies
  • Medical conditions (allergies, asthma, diabetes, stroke, seizure)
