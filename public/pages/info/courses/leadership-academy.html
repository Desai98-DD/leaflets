Leadership Academy

This unique and ground breaking Leadership Academy is for UFCW 401 members only. All members are welcome to apply.

The Academy transforms participants into workplace and community leaders and is unlike any previous courses or programs UFCW 401 has offered.

Participants focus on personal growth, self-reflection, feedback, and mentoring. There is also a focus on organizing, negotiations, health and safety, and understanding the role of a Union Labour Relations Officer (ULRO).

The Academy has three levels. Each level requires participants complete 5–8 courses (1–3 days each). Courses may be in person or online, and some travel may be required.
