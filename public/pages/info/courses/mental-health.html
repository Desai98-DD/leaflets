Mental Health Advocate

The strength of Local 401 is our members. As members stand up and support each other, our union grows strong.

This volunteer program is offered through our partnership with UFCW’s webCampus. Its goal is to deliver knowledge and skills required to share mental health resources with others in the workplace.

Graduates can

  • Provide relevant information to workers dealing with mental health challenges
  • Add a notable credential to your resume
  • Build solidarity at your work location by supporting co-workers

The program is entirely online and available 24/7. To graduate, participants will need to:

  • Enroll in the program
  • Complete all three courses in the Mental Health Matters series (recognized by the Canadian Mental Health Association)
  • Complete the program assignment
  • Watch the Mental Health Advocate webinars and successfully complete the quiz
