Shop Steward Training

Please note: “Changing your Workplace, Changing your World” and “Representation and Leadership in Hard Times” are prerequisites for this course.

Learn the principles of trade unionism and union thinking. Know how to represent yourself and your co-workers with management.

This course covers

  • The grievance procedure
  • Interviews, investigations, and note-taking
  • Introduction to contract language
  • Facing management
  • Bullying and violence in the workplace
  • Union myths, and common likes/dislikes of the union
  • Roles and responsibilities of your union and being a Steward
  • Organizing
  • Collective bargaining
  • Basic health and safety training
