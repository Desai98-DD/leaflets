Indigenous Rights & Activism

This course, offered through UFCW Canada’s webCampus, is a hybrid learning experience. You’ll benefit from group discussions with Indigenous activists and UFCW 401 staff.

You will learn at your own pace with online materials, then return to the main group for discussion. These live discussions are where you can share experiences, ask questions, and learn with dedicated facilitators.

UFCW 401 is committed to working with Indigenous, Métis, and Inuit people on Turtle Island. Local 401 has Indigenous activists who sit on the UFCW Canada Indigenous Subcommittee, working with members across Canada to build community, spread awareness, and support reconciliation.

Through this course you’ll get an introduction to Indigenous peoples, culture, and history. With this knowledge, we hope you continue forming allyships and spark a lifelong passion for learning about this complex history.
