Changing your Workplace, Changing your World

Important: This course is a prerequisite for all other Local 401 courses.

You’re part of a union; now what?

It’s not a trivial question. It can be challenging to wrap your head around a union the size of UFCW Local 401.

In what political work does the union engage? How does the union advance the rights of women and young workers? And what about engagement in the community?

This course introduces you to Alberta’s largest private-sector union and explores how member involvement is a vital part of UFCW Local 401’s success.
